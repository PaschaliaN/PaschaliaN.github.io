About Me

Paschalia Nsato

Site Designer

Hello there! My name is Paschalia Nsato. I am located in Ann Arbor, MI, but I am originally from Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. I would describe myself as an all-around creative person. I love art, design, and photography. I have been drawing ever since I was little, and I started doing client work photography in 2019. I do graduation, engagement, family events, beauty and children photoshoots. However, I am open to being contacted on any photography opportunity. I stopped after the pandemic hit in 2020, and I was living in Tanzania. I am now open for business again; you can contact me on the form on this page for any photoshoot opportunity.

I am a first-year doing a Master's of Information at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor. My focus is in User Experience Design and Research.
